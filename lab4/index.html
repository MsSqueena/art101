Task One: Everyday Pseudocode

For this part of the assignment, we where told to make a Pseudocode for an "everday" task. The task that my partner and I decided on was how to make lemon bars!

How To Make Lemon Bars

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 Degrees
  2. Grease a 9x13 pan
  3. Make the crust
    • In a bowl add 2c flour, 1/2c sugar, 1/4tsp salt
    • Add 1c butter
    • Mix until it's a fine crumb
  4. Press the mixture into a pan, flattened
  5. Bake until golden brown around the edges, 20 mins
  6. Make the filling:
    • In a bowl add 1 1/2c Sugar, 1/4c flour
    • Add 4 large eggs
    • Whisk everything together
    • Zest 2 large lemons
    • Halve the lemons and add the zest and juice to the mixture
    • Combine everything together
  7. Pour filling over the crust, bake for 20 mins
  8. Cool in the fridge for a minimun of 2 hours
    • Shift powdered suger on top if you like
  9. Cut into Squares and Enjoy!
